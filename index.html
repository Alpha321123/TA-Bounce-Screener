How to transfer your data between versions

All your portfolio, trade history, paper trades, and simulation data is stored in your browser. To carry it forward to a new version:

1. Open your current version
2. Go to Export data in the sidebar (or click ⬆️ Export data)
3. Save the .json file to your computer
4. Open the new version
5. Go to Import data and select the saved file
6. All your data is restored instantly ✓
Data is stored locally in your browser · No account required · Works offline after first load
